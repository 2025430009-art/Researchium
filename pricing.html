Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch or cancel my plan anytime? ▼
Yes — upgrade, downgrade or cancel at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. No penalties or hidden fees.
Is there a student discount? ▼
Students with a valid .edu or institutional email get 20% off Pro Scholar and Elite plans. Contact support@researchium.in to apply.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, Net Banking, Paytm, GPay, PhonePe, and no-cost EMI on select banks for annual plans.
What is the refund policy? ▼
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied within 7 days, email us and we'll refund 100% — no questions asked.
Do earned certificates expire? ▼
No. Certificates you earn are yours forever, even if you cancel your subscription. They are permanently linked to your Researchium profile.
Can I buy a single course instead of a plan? ▼
Yes! Individual course purchases are available for all paid courses. You get lifetime access to that course including all future updates.
