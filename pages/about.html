OUR STORY

Democratizing Research-Grade Education for Every Indian

Founded by IIT and IIM alumni, Researchium was built on a simple belief: every student in India deserves access to the deepest, most rigorous education — regardless of geography or economic background.

OUR MISSION

Learn Deeper. Think Sharper.

In 2021, Researchium was born in a small apartment in Hyderabad with one question: why should the quality of education depend on whether you live in Mumbai or Malkajgiri?

Today we serve students in every district of India — from Leh to Kanyakumari — delivering the same quality of instruction that used to only be available in premium coaching centres in metro cities.

We are not just an education platform. We are a research institution that teaches. Every course on Researchium is evidence-based, peer-reviewed by subject experts, and continuously updated based on student performance data.
